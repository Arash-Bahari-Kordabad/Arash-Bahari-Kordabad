Biography

Arash Bahari Kordabad is a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Sebastien Gros at the Department of Engineering Cybernetics of Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Norway. He received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from University of Tabriz and Sharif Universiry of Technology, respectively. His research interest centers in the field of MPC, Control Theory and Reinforcement learning.
